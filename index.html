About Cloud Native Days Italy

Cloud Native Days (CNS) Italy is a local, community-organized event that gathers adopters and technologists from open source and cloud native communities.

This is the fourth edition in Italy (formerly known as KCD Italy), aiming to bring the community together. The event provides a platform for professionals and experts from all levels and backgrounds to learn, network, and share their knowledge about cloud-native technologies.

What to Expect?

Networking

Meet and connect with like-minded professionals and experts from the cloud-native community.

Technical Talks

Learn from the best in the industry about the latest trends and technologies in the cloud-native space.

Workshops

Get hands-on experience with cloud-native technologies and tools in our workshops.
