The Event

About Cloud Native Days Italy

Cloud Native Days Italy is a community-driven event in Italy dedicated to cloud native and open source technologies.

Born from the passion of the Italian tech community, it brings together developers, adopters, and enthusiasts to connect, learn, and grow together. Now in its fifth edition, the event has become a reference point where people from all backgrounds and experience levels meet, share stories, and shape the future of cloud native in Italy; together.

What to Expect?

Technical Talks

Learn from the best in the industry about the latest trends and technologies in the cloud-native space.

Networking

Meet and connect with like-minded professionals and experts from the cloud-native community.

Workshops

Get hands-on experience with cloud-native technologies and tools in our workshops.
