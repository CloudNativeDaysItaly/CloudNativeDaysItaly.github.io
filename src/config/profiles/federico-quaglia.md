Federico Quaglia is a DevOps Engineer based in Florence, Italy, with nearly a decade of experience in cloud-native infrastructure and DevOps. Over the course of his career, he has grown into a seasoned platform engineering professional with deep expertise in Kubernetes, cloud platforms, and infrastructure automation.
Federico currently works at Onetag, where he manages physical servers and Kubernetes clusters, deploys and maintains core platform components — including MySQL, the ELK stack, Prometheus, VictoriaMetrics, Grafana, Aerospike, and HAProxy.
Besides he has developed custom Helm charts, Ansible roles and playbooks, and has built GoLang-based Kubernetes webhooks, including a custom mutating webhook for AWS pod identity management.
His cloud-native journey includes hands-on experience with Cloud Foundry on BOSH architecture, Rancher Kubernetes distributions, CI/CD pipeline design, and automated deployment of distributed systems including Kafka, RabbitMQ, Cassandra, and PostgreSQL.
Federico holds a Computer Science degree from the Università degli Studi di Perugia and is passionate about tackling complex infrastructure challenges and embracing new technologies.
