Mauricio Salatino, also known as salaboy, works for Dash0 as an OSS Ecosystem Engineer. He is a Java Champion, Cloud Native Ambassador, and international Keynote speaker. Before working at Dash0, Mauricio spent the last 10 years building tools for Cloud-Native developers at companies such as Red Hat and VMware. When he is not writing tools for developers or contributing to open source projects in the Cloud Native space, he teaches about Java, AI, Kubernetes and Cloud-Native on his Blog salaboy.com.
