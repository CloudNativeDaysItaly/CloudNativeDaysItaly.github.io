Massimo Bandinelli has been with Aruba since 2017 and serves as Marketing Manager for Aruba Cloud. He brings with him over 25 years of international experience in the ICT sector.

He began his career at the Dada Group as a System Administrator, then went on to become Head of the Information Technology team at Register.it, leading distributed teams across Europe. He later continued in product development as Manager of the Hosting division, playing a key role in the Agile/Scrum transition of the companies within the Register.it Group: first as Product Owner and then as Head of the Lean/Agile area.

Today, at Aruba, he combines technical expertise with a strategic marketing vision and is regarded as a key point of reference for Cloud products and solutions, as well as for the development of their related markets.
