Alessandro Liscio is a Machine Learning Engineer specialising in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Generative AI, with a strong focus on operationalising AI systems and improving their integration into real-world processes. His work centres on streamlining AI adoption, standardising deployment practices, and enhancing the reliability and scalability of machine learning pipelines across different domains.

He has experience designing and implementing AI-driven solutions, with a background that spans NLP-based automation, data processing, and predictive modelling. While his earlier work focused more on healthcare applications and decision-support systems, his current efforts are oriented towards building robust, production-ready AI systems and improving end-to-end workflows.

Alessandro holds a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bologna and a bachelor's degree in computer science from the University of Camerino. He has contributed to research in Artificial Intelligence in Medicine and presented his work at international conferences. He remains interested in advancing AI research, particularly in making complex models more usable, reliable, and impactful in practice.
