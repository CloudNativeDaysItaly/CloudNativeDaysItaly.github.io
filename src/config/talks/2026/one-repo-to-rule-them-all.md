Managing distributed Kubernetes infrastructures across multiple cloud providers — GCP, AWS, Alibaba Cloud — with a mix of shared and cluster-specific requirements is a significant challenge. In this talk, we present a production-proven approach used to manage diverse client clusters from a single management hub, leveraging Sveltos as the distribution engine and ArgoCD as the delivery layer for CRDs.
