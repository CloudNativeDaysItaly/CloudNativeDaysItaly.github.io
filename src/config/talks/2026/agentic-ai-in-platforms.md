Agentic AI is moving from experimental copilots to production-grade systems that reason, plan, and act across complex enterprise platforms. This talk explores how these patterns can be productized as cloud-native platform capabilities: agent runtimes, context layers, tool gateways, policy and evaluation pipelines, and orchestration planes.
