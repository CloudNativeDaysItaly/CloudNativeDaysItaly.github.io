We've all been there: you finally secure a high-end GPU for your cluster, only to realize that assigning a massive card to a single small pod is a huge waste of money. This session will break down how GPU sharing actually works and why it often doesn't. We'll explore how Dynamic Resource Allocation (DRA) combined with Multi-Instance GPU (MIG) enables true hardware isolation.
