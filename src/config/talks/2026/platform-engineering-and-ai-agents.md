In Platform Engineering, AI agents can speed up IaC management, but without clear boundaries, they can also amplify errors and operational impact. In this talk, we'll look at a spec-driven approach in which dedicated agents operate with specific identities, explicit guardrails, and a purpose-built toolchain.
