In a enterprise environment, building a knowledge mining tool is not just about artificial intelligence or building a chatbot — it requires a solid architectural foundation, strict governance, and seamless integration. This talk explores the journey of designing and implementing a conversational tool for knowledge mining, focusing on the architectural decisions and patterns that enabled compliance and scalability.
