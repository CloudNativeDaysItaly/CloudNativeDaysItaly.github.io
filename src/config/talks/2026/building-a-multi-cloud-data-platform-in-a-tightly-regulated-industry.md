In today's world data is king. It is everywhere, collected seemingly by everyone, yet so many industries lack a good data platform. Healthcare is one of them - and it gets even more difficult when you take into account the privacy aspects of health data. This talk will touch on how to enable innovative, AI-driven products when dealing with highly sensitive data, and when the market gives you all the tools you can imagine, how do you pick which ones you use?
