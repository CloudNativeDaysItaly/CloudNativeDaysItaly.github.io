Horizontal scaling is often the default response to increased load on Kubernetes, but it can mask deeper inefficiencies within the single pod. In this interactive "choose your own adventure" session, we will re-live our optimization journey. We'll show that true efficiency comes from the synergy between runtime settings and pod configurations, allowing us to reduce memory by 56% and costs by 30%.
