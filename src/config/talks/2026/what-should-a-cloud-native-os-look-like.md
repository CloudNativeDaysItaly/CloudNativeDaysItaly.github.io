Cloud-Native platforms are built around declarative APIs, reconciliation, and rapid change, yet the operating systems underneath them are often still upgraded in place, mutated over time, and treated as infrastructure details rather than part of the platform contract. In this talk, we'll look at the operating system through a cloud-native platform lens and explore what changes when cloud-native principles are applied all the way down to the OS.
